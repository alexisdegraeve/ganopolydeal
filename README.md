ğŸ® Rules (Ganopoly Deal Edition)

The rules are identical to Monopoly Deal, with a thematic twist:

Goal of the game
Collect 3 full property sets of different colors to win the game.

Types of cards

Property Cards: Represent Indian-themed locations or symbols.

Money Cards: Used to pay rent or as part of transactions.

Action Cards: Special powers like "Charge Rent", "Steal Card", or "Swap Property".

Setup

Shuffle all cards.

Deal 5 cards to each player.

The remaining deck becomes the draw pile.

Gameplay

On your turn, draw 2 cards from the deck.

Play up to 3 cards:

Place property cards in front of you.

Play action cards to affect opponents.

Pay or collect money using money cards.

End your turn; the next player goes.

Winning the game

The first player to collect 3 full property sets wins!

All classic Monopoly Deal rules apply; the difference is purely visual and thematic, inspired by Indian culture and Ganesh.
